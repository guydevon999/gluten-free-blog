Resources for eating gluten-free

Below are some links to helpful websites that should be of use to you whether you are just starting your gluten-free journey, or are simply looking for dinner ideas. Findmeglutenfree.com is a site that helps you search for restaraunts that have gluten-free offerings. You can often find recent reviews from users saying whether or not they recommend certain establishments. Beyondceliac.org contains a wealth of information about celiac disease, current research, and even recipes and food tips. Udisglutenfree.com is the website for the Udi's brand, which is dedicated to making gluten-free versions of common foods. Their products include bread, bagels, pizza crusts, and even frozen meals. The cleveland clinic article contains an exhaustive list of ingredients to avoid when on a gluten-free diet. And finally, theloopywhisk.com has wonderful recipes for gluten-free versions of just about anything you could dream of baking. There are also tips on how to adjust recipes to make them gluten-free, as well as recipes to accomodate people with other dietary restrictions like dairy-free or vegan.
